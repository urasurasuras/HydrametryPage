About us

Here at Hydrametry Software, we pride ourselves on quality, and the dedication it takes to
produce quality. Our vision is to create fun, accessible experiences that anyone can pick up and play.
All the while, we'll be curbing the stuff we all hate, be it microtransactions, "season passes",
or paid DLC that contains half the content of the game that got ripped out at launch. You only
pay the price you see.

Our team is comprised of nine people, all with unique talent in their respective areas, be they art, programming, or music.
