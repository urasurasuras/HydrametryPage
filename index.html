About us

Here at Hydrametry Software, we pride ourselves on quality, and the dedication it takes to
produce quality. Our vision is to create fun, accessible experiences that anyone can pick up and play.
All the while, we'll be curbing the stuff we all hate, be it microtransactions
or day-one DLC that contains half the content of the game that got ripped out at launch. You only
pay the price you see.

Our team is comprised of seven people, all with unique talent in their respective areas, be they art,
programming, or music. We want nothing but to bring joy to the people who play our games. After all, video
games are about having fun - and the more smiles we puton faces, the better.
